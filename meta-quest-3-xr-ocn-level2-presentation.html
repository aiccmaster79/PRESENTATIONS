What is XR?

XR (Extended Reality) is the umbrella term for immersive technologies that blend digital content with human experience—including VR, AR and Mixed Reality (MR).

VR — Virtual Reality

  • Fully digital environment
  • Headset blocks the real room
  • Great for simulation & games
  • Example: immersive training worlds

AR / MR — Augmented / Mixed

  • Digital overlays on the real world
  • Pass-through cameras / glasses
  • Great for guides & design
  • Quest 3 colour pass-through MR

Meet Meta Quest 3

Meta Quest 3 is a standalone XR headset—no PC required for most experiences—with higher resolution displays, mixed reality colour pass-through, and hand tracking.
